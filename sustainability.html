Sustainability

We are committed to reducing our impact on the environment and promoting sustainability. Here are some of the steps we are taking to make our event more sustainable:

  • Recycling and composting: We provide recycling and composting stations throughout the festival grounds to reduce the amount of waste that ends up in landfills. Please dispose of your waste properly and follow the signage at the waste stations.
  • Sustainable food and drink options: We work with food and drink vendors who use sustainable and locally sourced ingredients. We also encourage the use of reusable cups and plates to reduce single-use plastic waste.
  • Eco-friendly transportation: We encourage attendees to use public transportation, carpool, or bike to the festival. We also offer bike parking on-site and provide shuttle services from nearby transit stations.
  • Renewable energy: We use renewable energy sources such as solar power to reduce our reliance on fossil fuels.
  • Education and awareness: We provide information and resources to help educate our attendees about sustainability and how they can make a positive impact on the environment.

We believe that small actions can make a big difference and we encourage our attendees to join us in our sustainability efforts. Thank you for your cooperation and let's work together to make our music festival a sustainable and eco-friendly event!
