Safety

Your safety is our top priority, and we want to ensure that you have a safe and enjoyable experience while attending our event. Here are some important safety guidelines to keep in mind:

  • Stay hydrated: Make sure to drink plenty of water throughout the day to avoid dehydration. We provide free water stations throughout the festival grounds.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and footwear: Wear comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for outdoor activities. Bring a hat and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Be aware of your surroundings: Keep an eye out for any potential hazards, such as uneven ground or trip hazards. Report any safety concerns to a staff member or security personnel.
  • Follow the festival rules: We have specific rules in place to ensure the safety of all attendees. Follow the rules regarding prohibited items, smoking, and behavior. Failure to comply with these rules may result in removal from the festival grounds.
  • Stay with your group: It's always safer to stay with your friends or group. Make sure to establish a meeting point in case you get separated.
  • Use designated transportation: If you plan on drinking, use designated transportation such as a taxi or ride-sharing service. Do not drink and drive.
  • Seek medical attention if needed: If you or someone around you needs medical attention, seek help from a staff member or medical personnel. We have a first aid station onsite for minor injuries and illnesses.

By following these safety guidelines, we can ensure that everyone has a safe and enjoyable time at our music festival. Thank you for your cooperation and have a great time!
