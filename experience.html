Experience

Get ready to experience the ultimate summer music festival with Retro Records!
Welcome to Lost in Time Festival, a celebration of the best music from the 80s and 90s set in the heart of sunny California. Since 2013, our festival has been a must-attend event for music lovers who are ready to dance the day away. Located in the desert of California, our festival boasts a picturesque open field surrounded by palm trees, mountains, and endless blue skies. Come dressed in your most vibrant and colorful vintage outfits and join us for a day filled with non-stop music and entertainment! While some of the original artists may no longer be with us, our talented DJs will expertly curate sets featuring all your favorite hits from the era. Get ready to groove to the sounds of Madonna, Michael Jackson, Prince, and many more.

At Lost in Time Festival, we encourage our guests to leave their cell phones at home and fully immerse themselves in the magic of the moment.
Join us this summer for a one-of-a-kind festival experience that you'll never forget!
