Experience

Welcome to Lost in Time festival, the event celebrating the music of the 80s and 90s in beautiful California. A unique experience, where strict rules have been implemented to recreate the atmosphere of past colourful and offline festivals. During the two music evenings, guests are invited to leave their phones behind and fully immerse themselves in the music and magic of the festival. Between nostalgic music, delicious food and a festive atmosphere, Lost in Time is the must-see event for anyone who wants to revive the past decades. Do not miss the opportunity to take a jump back in time and enter the reality of the most engaging festival ever!

Rules and Dresscode

At Lost in Time Festival you can completely immerse yourself in the retro atmosphere, dancing on the notes of the great successes of the past and having fun with friends, but with one important rule: cell phones are forbidden! Let yourself be transported back in time, wearing colorful and extravagant clothes, just as it was in the 80s and 90s! Our mission is to make you revive old times, forget everyday worries and enjoy the excitement of music as you once did! Come with us on a nostalgic journey through time and don’t forget your colorful spirit!

Camping

The festival we propose is an unforgettable experience for all lovers of music and life in the open air. The location is immersed in nature and offers large green spaces where you can enjoy the performances of your favorite artists. To ensure maximum comfort for the participants, we have set up a dedicated area for the campsite, equipped with all the necessary facilities such as shower rooms, bathrooms and charging points for smartphones. This way you can experience the festival 24 hours a day, without having to worry about going home after the shows.
