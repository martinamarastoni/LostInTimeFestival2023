Frequently Asked Questions

IS THE FESTIVAL FAMILY FRIENDLY?

Yes, the festival is a family-friendly event with a designated kids' zone and activities suitable for all ages.

WHAT CAN I BRING WITH ME TO THE FESTIVAL?

You are welcome to bring a small bag or backpack with essentials such as sunscreen, water, and a hat. However, outside food and drinks are not allowed. The use of phones would be forbidden but bring one if you think you might need it for emergencies.

HOW CAN I SHOW MY TICKET IF I CAN'T BRING MY PHONE?

The use of phone to record or take pictures is forbidden, but it's not forbidden to carry your phone to show your ticket, to make emergency calls or in case of other necessities.

WHAT TIME DOES THE FESTIVAL START?

You will receive further information about time and location of the event once you buy your ticket.

CAN I BUY FOOD AND DRINKS AT THE FESTIVAL?

Yes, there will be a variety of food and drink vendors at the festival serving up delicious eats and refreshing beverages.

IS THERE PARKING AVAILABLE AT THE FESTIVAL?

Yes, there is ample parking available onsite. However, we recommend taking public transportation or carpooling to reduce traffic congestion.

CAN I BRING MY PETS TO THE FESTIVAL?

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed at the festival due to safety and hygiene reasons.

WHAT HAPPENS IF IT RAINS?

The festival is held rain or shine, but in the event of severe weather conditions, we may need to cancel or postpone the event for safety reasons.
